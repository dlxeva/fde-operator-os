# Mission Brief

- **Opportunity**: Inspection-to-remediation loop for property safety hazards
- **Operator mission**: Help supervisors move from inconsistent photo review to a more reliable hazard qualification and closure loop
- **Operational pain**: Hazards are reviewed inconsistently, remediation proof is weak, and overdue items are tracked outside the official system
- **Consequence if unresolved**: Repeat hazards, weak auditability, slower closure, and growing reporting overhead
- **Target outcome**: Reduce time from inspection submission to remediation ticket creation while improving evidence quality for closure
- **Accountable owner**: Regional safety supervisor
- **Success metric(s)**:
  - reduce median review-to-ticket time by 30%
  - increase closure records with acceptable photo evidence to 90%
  - reduce manually reconciled overdue items
- **Critical constraints**:
  - human approval remains required before ticket creation
  - existing ticketing tool remains system of record
  - phase-one scope must fit one inspection category
- **Go / no-go view**: Go, if one hazard category can be piloted with existing mobile form and ticketing workflow
- **Unknowns requiring validation**:
  - current false-positive rate for inspector submissions
  - whether closure evidence standards are consistent across supervisors
