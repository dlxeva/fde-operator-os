# System Problem Frame

- **Problem class**: Closed-loop operational workflow with perception-assisted qualification
- **System boundary**: Inspection submission, supervisor review, remediation ticket initiation, closure evidence review, and overdue visibility
- **Primary bottleneck**: Inconsistent supervisor qualification and weak evidence enforcement between detection and closure
- **North-star outcome**: A repeatable inspection-to-remediation loop with trusted review criteria and auditable closure evidence
- **Non-goals**:
  - replacing the facilities ticketing platform
  - full automation of hazard decisions
  - broad multi-category rollout in phase one
- **Critical assumptions**:
  - one hazard category has enough recurring volume to pilot
  - supervisors can align on a minimum acceptable evidence standard
  - ticket creation can be triggered through existing tooling without major rebuild
- **Deferred questions**:
  - whether additional model support is needed for closure verification
  - how to unify reporting across all buildings after phase one
