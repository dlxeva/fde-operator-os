# Reusable Patterns

## Problem Pattern

- Multi-step exception workflows often fail at the handoff between signal intake, task creation, and proof of closure.

## Workflow Template Candidate

- Human-reviewed exception qualification
- Task creation in system of record
- Evidence-based closure
- Overdue escalation with coordinator visibility

## Reuse Notes

- Reusable for operational exception loops where screenshot or form intake is only the first step.
